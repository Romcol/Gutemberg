Gutemberg project

Realization of collaborative platform applied to old newspapers

Presentation

This project is a part of the Computer Science department 4th year training course of INSA Rennes and proposed by the Intuidoc team of Irisa. Its aim is to improve the access to local and old press archives. So, our project concerns the provision of old press documents using an accessible to all platform. This is from a prototype, which is conceived by the Intuidoc team, we're developing this platform. This prototype allows to read into old newspapers images (19th and 20th centuries) to generate a structured representation of each page content. Moreover, Yoann Royer, a Sopra-Steria project manager and an INSA former graduate has been offering his support and advice during the project.

The access to the old documents in the digital age is a real problem

Several millions of newspaper being dated from the French revolution until today are currently in the archives. In general, the archives documents access is improvable. Indeed, this access is restricted for document conservation reasons. Moreover, some archives contain mileages of shelves and, even if this number is lower for old press, the access to documents which are about a precise topic is complicated. However, the digitization doesn't resolve totally the access problem. That needs also to make these documents accessible online with ergonomic tools.

Goals

Our platform goals and features are separated in three approaches :

Search

The user will easily obtain articles about a specific topic. Also, he will be able to refine the search with filter and sort tools. Last, the user will merely be able to find a newspaper particular edition.

So, our platform is provided with an efficient search engine which allows to search in the articles content. Also, this engine allows to filter the results and to sort regarding their publication date. The obtained articles with the search are posted with their title, newspaper name, date and a keywords centered extract. In the same way, a user can find a newspaper particular edition.

View

The newspapers viewing will be appropriate to big images and make easier the reading. Also, the newspaper and articles information will be posted. And well, the close articles will be proposed to the user to help him go into his search in-depth.

Our viewer post the page to avoid a too long loading. It allows to zoom in and zoom out the image. Overlays highlight the various articles and in particular the searched article. The newspaper information (name, date, titles list) and the selected article information are posted next to the viewer. Close articles are proposed regarding the searched keywords, the publication date ...

Contribute

This is as well essential to highlight the old press wealth. So, our platform offers a press review system. This is a set of articles which share a topic that the users want to highlight. Each user will be able to contribute to these reviews.

During the article viewing, the user can add the article to a press review. The list of reviews whom the user has already contributed is accessible. Also, it is possible to search among the reviews. The user can as well add the article to his favorites or add tags to it.
